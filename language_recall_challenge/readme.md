# Language Recall Challenge: User Guide 🚀

Welcome to the **Language Recall Challenge**! This app is a high-speed training tool designed to help you build instant language reflexes. Instead of slowly translating in your head, this "sprint" method trains your brain to recall phrases automatically.

---

## 🎮 How to Use the App

### 1. The Topic Manager (Your Library)
Before you start, you need to pick what you want to practice.
* **Select a Topic:** Use the dropdown menu to pick a category like "Family & Home" or "Restaurant & Dining."
* **Customize:** You can edit the phrases in the text box or type in your own using the `English | Spanish` format.
* **Save:** If you make changes, click **Save/Update** to store them in your browser.

### 2. Configure Your Session
Adjust the toggles to change the difficulty:
* **UI Language:** Switch the buttons and labels between English and Spanish.
* **Practice Mode:** * **ES Prompts:** You see Spanish and type English.
  * **EN Prompts:** You see English and type Spanish.
  * **Random:** The app switches directions constantly to keep you on your toes!
* **Timer:** Turn this on to see how many phrases you can finish in 60 seconds.
* **Audio Only:** The ultimate challenge! The text becomes invisible, forcing you to rely entirely on your listening skills.

---

## 🏃 The Sprint (Gameplay)

1. **Start:** Click **Start Sprint**.
2. **Recall:** Look at the prompt (or listen to the audio).
3. **Type:** Type your translation into the input box.
4. **Submit:** Press **Enter**.
   * **Correct ✅:** A green banner appears, and your score goes up!
   * **Wrong ❌:** A red banner appears, a buzzer sounds, and the correct answer is revealed.
5. **Next:** Press **Enter** again to move to the next phrase.

> **Pro Tip:** You can press **ESC** at any time to quit early and view your results.

---

## 📊 The Performance Audit

When the session ends, you will see a detailed **Performance Audit**. This table shows:
* Every phrase you practiced.
* How many times you got it **Right**.
* How many times you got it **Wrong**.

Use this audit to identify your "trouble phrases" so you can focus on them in your next session.

---

## 💡 Tips for Rapid Mastery

* **Speak it Out Loud:** Don't just type the answer—say it out loud as you type. This connects your typing reflex to your speaking reflex.
* **Listen to the Dialect:** The app is optimized for clear pronunciation. Pay attention to the rhythm and flow of the Colombian Spanish voices.
* **Embrace the Buzzer:** Don't get frustrated by mistakes! The buzzer is a "data point" for your brain that helps you remember the correct version more clearly next time.
* **Try Audio Only:** Once you feel confident with a topic, turn on **Audio Only**. This is the best way to prepare for real-world conversations where there are no subtitles!
